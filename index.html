TIMECARD VALIDATORX

Validate up to three clock-in/out periods per day for as many as four Saturday–Friday workweeks. Each week is checked independently for weekly hours, daily hours, required days off, and missing 30-minute breaks.

Workweek
Rules used for each Saturday–Friday week: maximum 40 paid hours/week; maximum 8 paid hours/day; at least one day off; periods from 5½ through 6 hours with less than a 30-minute break receive a warning, and longer periods receive an error.
