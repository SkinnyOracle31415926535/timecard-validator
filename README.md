# Timecard Validator

A small, dependency-free timecard validator for up to four consecutive Saturday–Friday workweeks. Each day supports up to three clock-in/out periods, with an optional unpaid break for every period. Periods automatically sort by clock-in time.

## Live page

[Open the Timecard Validator](https://skinnyoracle31415926535.github.io/timecard-validator/)

## Add to Home Screen

On iPhone or iPad, open the live page in Safari, choose **Share**, then choose **Add to Home Screen**. The installed app uses the green Timecard Validator icon.

## Checks

- Up to 40 paid hours per week
- Up to 8 paid hours per day
- At least one day off in each Saturday–Friday workweek
- A warning for periods from 5½ through 6 hours with less than a 30-minute unpaid break
- An error for periods longer than 6 hours with less than a 30-minute unpaid break

Everything runs locally in the browser. No entered schedule data is uploaded or stored.
